PART 1: EU LEGAL FRAMEWORK

1.1 Regulatory Landscape Overview

Regulation Purpose Key Feature for S2S
AIFMD Establishes the comprehensive framework for all Alternative Investment Funds. Defines the **Sub-Threshold AIFM** (S2S's target size).
EuVECA Specialist, voluntary regime for VCs investing in SMEs. Provides the **EU Marketing Passport** and **Simplified Compliance**.

1.3 EuVECA Key Requirements

Product Requirements (The Fund)
  • Investment Focus: At least 70% invested in Qualifying SMEs.
  • Qualifying SME: Employing fewer than 499 persons.
  • Compliance Timing: 70% calculation applies at the time of the initial investment only.
Manager & Investor Requirements
  • Capital: Minimum â‚¬50,000 initial capital.
  • Leverage: Strictly prohibited beyond uncalled commitments.
  • Investors: Only Professional Investors and Qualifying HNWIs (â‚¬100K+ commitment).

1.4 Key Benefit: EU Marketing Passport

Once registered, the manager can market across all EU/EEA member states via a simple **notification procedure** to the home regulator (CSSF), with no approval required from host member states. Marketing can commence after a 20 working day waiting period.
